Outlines a recent publication, in which we employed a computational model of genetic regulation to investigate whether the assortativity of a GRN inﬂuences its robustness and evolvability upon gene birth.
Conclusion:
Robustness of a GRN generally increases with increasing assortativity, while its evolvability generally decreases and this results in an increased proportion of assortative GRNs that are simultaneously robust and evolvable.
